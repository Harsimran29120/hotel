TRAFFIC NEWS

We want to remember that, due to the flood, the Colle di Tenda Tunnel and the Roya Valley road from Ventimiglia are interrupted, the only way to reach Limone from Liguria and France (and from Nice Cote d'Azur-NCE) is via the highway Torino-Savona or from Colle di Nava. Don't hesitate to contact us for any questions and suggestions that you need.
In summer, only with suitable vehicles (off-road), it is possible to reach us via military dirt mountain roads (contact us for more information).
For those arriving from the rest of Italy, no traffic problems.


Hotel L'ARTISIN is one of the most recent Bed & Breakfast in Limone Piemonte, with eleven confortable rooms and family rooms.

The B&b is located near "Maneggio" (330 mt.), site of the ski school, and at 1300 mt from the central square and the pedestrian street of the village.
The Hotel it's surrended by the nature and the green, in a sunny and peaceful position. From each room, you can enjoy the spectacular view of the mountains fron the sunrise to the sunset.

L'ARTISIN is a family hotel and the owner, T. Dalmasso, will be happy to welcome you and gives you all the best advises for make your holiday the best possible.
