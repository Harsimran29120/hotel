With over 80 km of ski slopes, the Riserva Bianca ski area has 3 different access points: Limone Piemonte, Limonetto and Quota 1400.
Here you come to do ski school, as well as to test yourself on the most challenging tracks. Because the district of Limone Piemonte is suitable for beginners as well as for experts and even for those who love off-slopes.
Developing between 1050 and 2100 meters of altitude, it enjoys natural snowfall throughout the winter season.
The modern ski lifts, built for the 2006 Turin Winter Olympics, make the Riserva Bianca a true skiing paradise. And there are many services for those who are approaching skiingfot the first time as an area enclosed with treadmills and games for children and, above all, with a ski school that is one of the best in Piedmont.




COME TO LIMONE TO BECAME A SKIER!
Located in the heart of the Riserva Bianca, the Limone Piemonte Ski School counts on the professionalism of 130 highly qualified and specialized instructors in the most varied skiing disciplines such as ski, carving, snowboard, telemark or freeride. The philosophy of our ski school is based on a complete offer of seasonal courses, lessons and activities, able to meet all the needs of small and large skiers. A service that help people with disabilities has also been developed, so everyone can enjoy the beauty of snow.
