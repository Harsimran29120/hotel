...is a nice tourist mountain village near Cuneo at about 1100 meters of altitude, considered the "pearl of the Provincia Granda". It's located in a strategic position, a few km from the Colle di Tenda and the Italian-French border. Surrounded by mountains, Limone P.te boasts a magnificent and modern ski area, "La Riserva Bianca", which enjoys about 80 km of slopes suitable for all types of skiers.
...what are waiting for to come to visit it?


In summer the paths, the old forts and the military roads of our valley are a destination for hikers on foot and/or by MTB. Don't miss "La Via del Sale", a splendid itinerary between 1800 and 2100 mt. that connects Limone-Monesi through suggestive places.(accessible on foot, by bike and by motorized means).

In addition, the Ligurian and the French Riviera can be reached in an hour by car or also by train.
